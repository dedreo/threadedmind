The screenshot that started it

This project began with an informal experiment. After encountering a social media post encouraging users to prompt ChatGPT to “roast” an ex-partner, I tried the interaction myself, without expectation of anything beyond novelty. The response was notably different from what I anticipated, and that moment prompted me to preserve the exchange. What follows grew out of that initial decision to save, rather than discard, the interaction. It reflects both how I was using the tool at the time and how the system responded before boundaries were clearly understood on either side.
